Using Nu-Con is fast and simple. Follow the directions below and you can change your image size in no time!

  1. First Select what mobile Operating System you need to make icons for.
  2. After selecting the OS, choose your image resolution from the drop-down menu.
  3. Then upload the image you would like to change.
  4. You're done! Right click "save image as" and download it!
  5. Tell all your friends about Nu-Con!
